Concept

We want to help others to eat better

Paleo.. What ?

The Paleolithic diet was (re)introduced few years ago to balance our modern food habits. This diet is essentially based on the consumption habits of our hunter-gatherer ancestors.

In simple words, this diet allows us to eat : meat, seafood, fruits, vegetables, eggs, nuts ands seeds. If you want a more precise list, you can check this one.

Well goodbye beloved dairy products, potatoes, sugar, processed foods and cereals (no more bread, pasta or rice).

We made our meals acoording to these strict rules, but remember you have to follow the diet that work most for you (balance your diet to make it work for you).

Meals for everybody available at gyms

Of course this diet can suit every lifestyle and habits, excluding specific needs (dairy for children). But it's in the sportive community that this diet has prooved its attractiveness.

That's the reason why we first want to focus on the delivery of gyms during lunch time. The work-life balance can be hard to define these days, and when you include sports, cooking can quickly become a challenge.

We simply try to help people focus more on what's matters without forgetting to eat healthy. (à revoir)
